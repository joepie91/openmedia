What is the Open Media Project?

The Open Media Project is a - for now - small project to promote the distribution of freely licensed video materials. Usually this will be videos under Creative Commons or similar licenses. All videos on this site can be distributed freely in their original form for non-profit purposes, and some videos may also allow you to distribute them commercially (do some research to figure this out).

How do I start?

Pick a category, and select a movie, documentary, talk or episode!

How do I help?

You can help by either submitting videos, or by donating through the Cryto Coding Collective.
